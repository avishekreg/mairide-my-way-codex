Offers and Requests: same logic, two entry points

Drivers post supply. Travelers post demand. MaiRide aligns both with route + time intelligence.

Driver Flow

Use Offer a Ride, set origin, destination, fare, seat count, and departure day/time (3-day window).

Traveler Flow

Use Request a Ride to post preferred route and fair offer for the same 3-day planning horizon.

Matching

Both sides see matching opportunities and can move directly into negotiation without leaving the dashboard.

Decision Ready

Departure date/time and route context stay visible so both parties can avoid booking confusion.

Pro Tip: Keeping route names clear and realistic significantly improves match quality.
