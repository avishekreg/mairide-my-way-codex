From counter offer to completed ride

This is the full trust-and-transaction flow you should expect in production usage.

1. Negotiation starts

Either side can send a counter offer. Loop remains active until accepted or rejected.

2. Offer accepted

Booking enters confirmed path and platform fee panel is activated.

3. Platform fee payment

Pay online or hybrid with Maicoins (within limit). GST applies on net cash component.

4. Contact unlock

Once payment is successful, contact details become available for coordination.

5. Ride completion + review

When ride is ended, post-ride rating flow is triggered for both traveler and driver.

Pro Tip: If a booking is under active negotiation, avoid canceling the ride to keep negotiation state consistent.
