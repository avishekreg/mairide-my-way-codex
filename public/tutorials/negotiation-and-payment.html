Negotiation, Payment, and Completion Tutorial

End-to-end ride lifecycle from offer to post-ride review.

  1. Traveler or driver sends a counter offer.
  2. Negotiation remains live until one side accepts or rejects.
  3. After acceptance, platform fee payment options appear.
  4. Hybrid mode supports Maicoins usage up to configured cap.
  5. Contact details unlock post successful platform fee confirmation.
  6. After ride completion, rating/review flow is triggered.
