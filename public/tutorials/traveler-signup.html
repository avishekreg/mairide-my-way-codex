Traveler Signup Tutorial

Create your traveler account in under 2 minutes.

  1. Open signup and choose Traveler.
  2. Enter full name, email, mobile, password.
  3. Add referral code (optional) and accept required declarations.
  4. Verify email OTP, then verify phone OTP.
  5. Account opens directly into traveler dashboard.
