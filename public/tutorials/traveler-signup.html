Traveler Signup in 5 clean steps

No friction. No confusion. Just a secure path to your first booking.

1Open Sign Up and choose Traveler.
2Enter full name, email, +91 mobile, and password.
3Add referral code (optional) and accept declarations.
4Verify Email OTP, then Phone OTP.
5Land in dashboard and start searching rides.
Pro Tip: Keep your preferred route and timings ready before your first search to get faster matches.
