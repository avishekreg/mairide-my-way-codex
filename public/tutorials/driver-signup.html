Driver onboarding and approval flow

Designed for trust. Built for speed. Complete once, then start earning.

1Choose Driver during signup.
2Fill identity, vehicle, insurance, and contact details.
3Upload required documents and declaration consent.
4Click Complete Setup to submit for review.
5Admin approves from Pending Verification. Driver dashboard unlocks fully.
Pro Tip: Clear document photos and valid expiry dates speed up approval time dramatically.
