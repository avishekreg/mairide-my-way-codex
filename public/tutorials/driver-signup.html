Driver Signup & Verification Tutorial

Complete onboarding and move to admin verification safely.

  1. Choose Driver in signup mode.
  2. Complete identity, vehicle, insurance, and document uploads.
  3. Accept declarations and click Complete Setup.
  4. Your profile appears in Pending Verification for admin review.
  5. After approval, driver dashboard features are unlocked.
