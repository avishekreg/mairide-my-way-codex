MaiRide Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 7, 2026

This policy explains how MaiRide collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data while operating as a ride aggregation technology platform.

1. Data we collect

  • Identity and profile: name, email, phone, role, profile image.
  • Driver verification data: submitted document metadata and related media.
  • Ride data: route points, booking lifecycle, negotiation amounts, ride status.
  • Payment metadata: gateway IDs, order IDs, amounts, status, timestamps (not card CVV/PIN).
  • Support and safety: tickets, feedback, ratings, moderation and audit events.
  • Device/session data: logs required for reliability, fraud prevention, and abuse control.

2. How we use data

  • Account creation, authentication, and platform access control.
  • Ride discovery, matching, negotiations, and booking operations.
  • Payment processing and transaction reconciliation.
  • Customer support, dispute handling, safety monitoring, and policy enforcement.
  • Service reliability, analytics, and product improvements.

3. Platform model disclosure

MaiRide is an aggregator platform. We do not operate vehicles, employ drivers for transport operations, or act as a transport contractor. Data exchange between matched parties is necessary to enable service fulfillment and support workflows.

4. Legal basis and consent

We process data based on contractual necessity, legitimate business interest, legal compliance, and user consent where required.

5. Data sharing

  • With matched traveler/driver parties when required for confirmed rides.
  • With payment gateway and infrastructure vendors under contractual safeguards.
  • With law enforcement/regulators when legally required.

6. Data retention

We retain data for operational, legal, accounting, anti-fraud, and dispute-resolution needs. Retention windows vary by data type and legal obligations.

7. Security practices

We apply access control, role-based permissions, transport security, and audit logging. No internet system is absolutely risk-free; users should also protect device and account credentials.

8. Your rights

  • Access and update profile data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate records.
  • Request deletion where legally permissible.
  • Raise data/privacy concerns via in-app support.

9. Cookies and local storage

MaiRide uses essential local storage and cookies for session state, language preference, and app reliability features (including install behavior and core UX continuity).

10. Third-party services

Third-party APIs (maps, payments, messaging, and AI) may process limited data required for requested actions. Their own policies apply in addition to MaiRide’s controls.

11. Children

Service is not intended for underage users under applicable law.

12. Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy over time. Material updates are reflected by revised effective date.

13. Contact

Use in-app support for privacy requests and include relevant account/booking details for faster processing.
