Pain points MaiRide solves

For travelers:

  • Hard to find reliable long-distance options at fair rates.
  • Last-minute uncertainty and poor pricing transparency.
  • No clear way to negotiate safely with verified context.
  • Difficulty planning future-day travel with confidence.

For drivers:

  • Return trips with empty seats burn fuel without revenue.
  • No structured way to discover demand in advance.
  • Low visibility into true profitability per leg.
  • Fragmented communication and booking proof flow.

Maicoins, referrals, and tier value

  • Maicoins: Used to reduce platform fee (within configured limits) so repeat users spend less.
  • Referral model: Users get referral codes and unlock rewards as referred users complete real ride activity.
  • Tier progression: Engagement quality and repeat activity improve wallet value and long-term savings.
  • Costing model: Service fee is intentionally small vs typical ride economics, making adoption easier for both sides.

Why MaiRide stands out vs alternatives

  • Empty-leg first: not just dispatching new rides, but optimizing rides already happening.
  • User-centric negotiation: both sides can negotiate until accepted or rejected.
  • Operational trust: onboarding, records, support tickets, and admin visibility are built in.
  • Environmental upside: better seat utilization lowers avoidable fuel burn per completed rider-trip.
✅ Better occupancy means lower wastage on existing journeys.

Getting started is simple

  • Sign up as traveler or driver.
  • Complete profile and verification where required.
  • Search rides or post/request rides in your sector.
  • Negotiate, confirm, pay platform fee, and complete the trip.
  • Submit post-ride review to strengthen trust for the community.
