1. Platform role and scope

MaiRide provides discovery, matching, booking workflow, negotiation tools, support workflows, ratings, and payment collection for platform-fee components. Ride execution is performed by the Driver and Traveler directly. MaiRide does not control vehicle operation, route choices, or on-road conduct.

3. Driver onboarding and verification

Drivers must complete profile setup, document uploads, and verification checks. Approval is at MaiRide’s discretion based on safety and compliance criteria. MaiRide may suspend or reject applications for incomplete, false, or unverifiable information.

4. Bookings, negotiations, and ride commitments

  • Fare negotiations are binding only once accepted by the relevant party in-app.
  • Platform fee payments are required to unlock contact details and complete booking commitment steps.
  • Once a ride is confirmed, cancellation rights are restricted per policy and may require support intervention.

5. Pricing, fees, GST, and MaiCoins

  • Ride fare is agreed between Traveler and Driver.
  • Platform fee and GST are displayed in-app at booking stage.
  • Where hybrid payment is enabled, GST applies on the net cash platform-fee component as configured by the platform logic.
  • MaiCoins are promotional utility units and not legal tender.

6. Refund policy

Refunds are handled case-by-case based on transaction status, fraud checks, and service events.

  • If payment is debited but booking does not get confirmed due to verified platform error, eligible platform-fee reversal/refund may be processed.
  • If a confirmed ride is cancelled due to driver/traveler fault, refunds may be partially or fully declined based on policy and evidence.
  • Convenience, gateway, or statutory charges may be non-refundable where applicable.
  • Refund timelines depend on payment gateway and bank rails.

7. Dispute resolution
